Othello Development Kit

Introduction

The ODK lets your C++ Othello program play automatically on GGS (the Generic Game Server)

  • Play against many of the world's top computer and human players
  • Nice GUIs are available for GGS, you can use one as your front end

If you already have a program, it could be playing on GGS in under 15 minutes.

Using the ODK

Most users should only need 'Prerequisites' and the 'Quick Start Guide' to get their program on GGS.
